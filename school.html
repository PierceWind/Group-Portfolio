DFCAITTI

For the academic year 2019-2020, the Dr. Filemon C. Aguilar Memorial College of Las Pias – Institute of Technology is offering 500 scholarship spaces for its new information system course.

The Bachelor of Science in Information System (BSIS) programme was first opened this school year after the Commission on Higher Education authorized the municipal government's application for the DFCAMCLP-IT to offer the four-year study, according to Mayor Imelda Aguilar.

The new course complements the city college's existing four-year offerings, which include Bachelor of Science in Accountancy, Financial Management, Human Resource Management, and Marketing.

Every school year, DFCAMCLP enrolls a total of 1,000 scholars in various courses, with the goal of producing certified public accountants who are among the Top 10 board scorers.

Las Pias will continue to provide free and high-quality education from pre-school to college, including technical and vocational training courses, as well as financial aid to help students find work.

Professsional in Networking

This course introduces students to the fundamentals of computer communications, telecommunications, and networking. It gives a general overview of both local and wide area networks. Processes, protocols, networks, design, and a basic overview of the internet are also covered.
